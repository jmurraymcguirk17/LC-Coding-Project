Smart Lights

Our smart lights turn themselves on and off, making them extremely useful for people with mobility related disabilities and those who may have difficulty turning lights on and off with a traditional light switch, as well as providing general ease-of-use for all users.
The user can set pre-determined settings including the Light Level at which they want their lights to turn on, the light level they want their lights to be at.
In our user hub users can also view the current light level that their lights are outputting currently.
